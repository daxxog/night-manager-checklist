# Manager - Night Shift Checklist

## Shift Change
- [ ] Plan - Daily Schedule & Deployment Guide
- [ ] TIPTOP - Talk Into Position
- [ ] Cash management - skim / tills / verify petty cash

---

## Dinner Post Rush / Pre-Close Plan
- [ ] Plan - Daily Schedule & Deployment Guide
- [ ] TIPTOP - Talk Out of Position
- [ ] Banking complete
- [ ] Desk / Team area clean & organized
- [ ] Freezer / Cooler - product off floors, wrapped & dated accurate beef supply
- [ ] Team Closing Checklists follow-up

### BACKROOM (Manager responsibilities)
- [ ] Thermometers / probes clean & placed in roasts or stored properly
- [ ] Oven gloves clean, safe & stored properly
- [ ] Thermometers clean & calibrated
- [ ] Food & Paper products stored properly
- [ ] Soda boxes full / CO2 level

---

## Closing Management Plan
- [ ] Equipment off
- [ ] Outside lights/sign/music off (security lights on)
- [ ] Back door firmly closed
- [ ] Lock doors & DT window
- [ ] EOD Task List - POS / Cash Mgt. / Waste & Food Mgt.
- [ ] $ per roast for next day
- [ ] Set up next days Beef Sheet
- [ ] Validate overnight cooking (CNH)
- [ ] Complete Beef Sheet
- [ ] Team Closing Checklists complete
- [ ] Exhaust system off
- [ ] Inventory
- [ ] HVAC adjusted to night temp (as applicable)
- [ ] Alarm system armed / leave safely

---

## Waste Tracking
- [ ] Record inventory waste & menu items on tracking sheet at shift change (AM) & at close (PM)
- [ ] Enter waste amounts into the BOS accordingly
