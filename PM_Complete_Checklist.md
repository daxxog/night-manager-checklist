## Team Closing Checklist

### 24 hour restaurants follow all steps with the exception of the (*) items

---

### FRONTLINE / LOBBY

#### 3 HOURS BEFORE CLOSE
- [ ] Front Counters - including behind equipment & under-counter shelving. Clean, stocked & organized
- [ ] Tables / Seats / Highchairs
- [ ] Condiment / Beverage Sauces / nozzles - clean & stocked
- [ ] Parking Lot & DT lane (daylight hours only)
- [ ] Turnover Display - clean. Do not remove racks prior to dining room closing
- [ ] Recheck Dining Room:
  - [ ] Tables & seats clean
  - [ ] Condiment stand/sauce nozzles - clean & stocked
  - [ ] Trash receptacles - clean inside/out
  - [ ] Restrooms - clean & stocked
  - [ ] Windows, doors & entryway
  - [ ] Floors - sweep, dry mop & spot clean carpet

#### 2 HOURS BEFORE CLOSE
- [ ] Trash Receptacles - clean inside / out
- [ ] Windows / Walls / Doors - spot clean
- [ ] Registers / Monitors - clean
- [ ] Front Cooler(s) - clean inside/out & stocked / organized
- [ ] Restrooms - clean / stocked
- [ ] Service Walls - clean
- [ ] Vacuum / Sweep / Dry Mop - (clean carpet only if no guests present)

#### 1 HOUR BEFORE CLOSE
- [ ] Shake Machine - clean; outside, syrup area, canisters or pumps & fill syrups 1" from the top
- [ ] Coffee / Tea / Brew Pans - clean outside, spray head / replace right away. Tea in pitcher at DT until Close
- [ ] Floors - sweep, scrub & mop in 10' x 10' section for guest safety

#### DINING ROOM
- [ ] Vacuum - clean, check bag/change belt as needed & vacuum (vacuum only if no guests present)

#### AFTER CLOSE
- [ ] *5 Minutes prior to close – turn shake machine to wash cycle
- [ ] *Turn off Necessary Equipment
- [ ] *DT Drink Station - Clean
- [ ] *Put Away Leftover Product
- [ ] *Track Waste – toss expired products
- [ ] Coffee Pots / Au jus warmer - clean
- [ ] Shake Machine - clean & sanitize Wed. & Sun.
- [ ] Lemonade Bubbler - clean & sanitize Tues. & Sat. Discard remaining lemonade from machine to break bacteria cycle
- [ ] Dishes – clean & scrub sinks/warewasher
- [ ] Trash - place near back of restaurant
- [ ] Floors - sweep, scrub & mop in 10' x 10' section for safety

---

### BACKLINE / KITCHEN

#### 3 HOURS BEFORE CLOSE
- [ ] Back Room - Tables/shelving clean & organized
- [ ] Hand Sinks - clean and stocked
- [ ] Walk-in Coolers - clean, stock & organize
- [ ] Trash - before dark, ensure dumpster lids & gate are closed

#### 2 HOURS BEFORE CLOSE
- [ ] Ice machine - wash, rinse & sanitize including ice scoop/bucket
- [ ] Reach-in Coolers - clean, stock & organize
- [ ] Thermometers / Probes & thermometer pan - clean & sanitize
- [ ] Holding Ovens - clean inside/out then replace product
- [ ] Fry freezer - clean inside & out, stock / organize
- [ ] Secondary Toaster - clean
- [ ] Microwaves - clean inside & out
- [ ] Backline area - clean & stock, but remain set up until close
- [ ] FIFO bottles - Clean dispensing tips, lids, lid wraps, and use-by bands according to the bottle cleaning procedures

#### 1 HOUR BEFORE CLOSE
- [ ] Sandwich Chute & Counter - clean
- [ ] Fry Station Pre-wash & returned to place, use until closed
- [ ] Filter Pan clean & left to air dry
- [ ] Cook 'n Hold - pre-heat as applicable
- [ ] Hot Holding Unit - clean & closed only if cooking to order
- [ ] Scales - clean & calibrate beef / prep / lbs
- [ ] Waste Bins - emptied & cleaned
- [ ] Beef - Overnight cooking
- [ ] Slicer - Pre-clean wearing PPE (Slicer must remain set up & used until close)
- [ ] Heat lamp - clean

#### AFTER CLOSE
- [ ] *Turn off Equipment
- [ ] *Discard Expired Product - track waste
- [ ] *Put Away Leftover Product - Wrap open buns & products
- [ ] FIFO Sauces - discard all expired sauces
- [ ] *Beef - wrap in plastic, place in holding oven, ensure temp of oven is 150°F
- [ ] *Heat 'n Hold - turn off, clean trays & metal seal
- [ ] *BIB cheese - into holding oven (as applicable)
- [ ] Cheese dispenser - clean
- [ ] Toaster – wipe Teflon sheets, belt & wash parts
- [ ] Fry station - final wipe down
- [ ] Slicer - follow 4 hour cleaning
- [ ] Stainless Steel Safety Gloves & Cleaning Brushes - clean
- [ ] Cutting Boards, Food & Hand Contact Surfaces - follow 4 hour cleaning process
- [ ] Towels & Scrub Pads - dirty towel bucket overnight or min. 8 hours
- [ ] Sanitizer Spray Bottles - empty
- [ ] Trash - Tie all bags, consolidate & place all trash by back door
- [ ] Floors - sweep, scrub & mop, get under all equipment & tables
